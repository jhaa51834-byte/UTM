Features

Everything your marketing team needs for UTM management

🏗️

UTM Builder

Destination URL input with query string & fragment preservation, dropdowns for source/medium, unlimited custom parameters, and live validation.

🤖

AI Naming Assistant

Free text → suggested UTMs with mandatory user approval. Works offline via rules engine; uses Claude when an API key is set.

📋

Bulk CSV Processing

Upload CSV with URL, Source, Medium, Campaign columns. Per-row validation with export to CSV or Excel.

🛡️

Governance Rules

Admin-defined combination rules (e.g., google → cpc/organic only). Error rules block generation with explicit override.

📦

Templates & History

Save/reuse team presets. Full history with user, date, campaign tracking. Search & filter capabilities.

🔗

Short Links & QR Codes

TinyURL (no key) and Bitly (token) integration. PNG & SVG QR code download with copy-to-clipboard.
